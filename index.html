Minesweeper

Welcome to Minesweeper!

There are mines hidden throughout the board. If you click on a mine, it will explode, and that's game over.

Your goal is to uncover all the "safe" fields. Once all the fields without bombs have been uncovered, you win the game.

Left-click on the field will open/uncover the field, right-click will flag the field. You can use the flag if you suspect there is a bomb on a particular field.

If a field has adjacent bombs (immediate neighbor cells in all directions), you will see a number. The number tells you exactly how many adjacent cells are bombs.

If you click on a blank/safe field without adjacent bombs, it will uncover all continuous safe fields, until it reaches cells that have adjacent bombs.

To play automatic solvers, click the button below. Solvers will play the game 100,000 times, and you will see how many times the game was won!
